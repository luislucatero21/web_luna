About us

Mexicious Food is a fast-food Mexican-style restaurant. In Mexicious Food you can enjoy typical dishes like Quesadillas (With or without cheese), guaraches, pambazos, tamales, gorditas, garnachas, salbutes and many others.

Mexicious Food is not like any other Mexican food restaurant, what sets us apart from others is that we have band music from Monday to Thursday and mariachi from Friday to Sunday. Feel like in Mexico from the comfort of any country you are in. Like one of our client said: The best things of Mexico in just one place!

Our restaurant is among the top 10 of North America and among the first 100 worldwide. Do not wait any longer and come visit us Remember that on Thursdays we also have pozole!

Check our Menu:

Pambazo
Light brown wheat bread soaked in guajillo peppers and stuffed with potatoes and chorizo.
Pozole
Stew consisting of a very spicy broth, whose main ingredients are grains of tender corn, chili and pork or chicken.
Quesadillas
A tortilla that can be filled with cheese (and other ingredients) and heated.
Guaraches
Masa dough and smashed pinto beans placed in the center before it’s given the oblong shape, fried masa base, with a variety of toppings including green or red salsa, onions, potato, cilantro and any manner of protein such as ground beef or tongue, and then finished with queso fresco.
Tamal
Cornmeal dough stuffed with meat, chicken, chili or other ingredients, wrapped in corn or banana cob leaves and steamed or baked.
Gorditas
Made with corn or wheat dough, stuffed with pork rinds, beans or other ingredients.
Garnachas
Thick corn tortilla with a raised edge made from corn that is fried by oil. It is accompanied with a delicious sauce, beans, chopped onion, cream of milk and cheese.
Salbutes
Puffed deep fried tortilla that is topped with lettuce, sliced avocado, pulled chicken or turkey, tomato and pickled red onion
